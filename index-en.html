Our Mission

Helping children love nature, one story at a time.

Bella the Bee

A curious little bee discovers why bees are so important for the planet.

Through her colorful adventures, Bella learns how bees carry pollen and help flowers grow.

A gentle and bright book that helps children understand how every small action can help protect living things.

Bibo the Beaver

Bibo the Beaver and the Wetland Guardians takes children on an educational adventure into the heart of ponds and biodiversity.

Young readers will discover the importance of beavers, wetlands and protecting nature.

A tender and educational book that teaches children to love, observe and protect the small treasures of our planet.
